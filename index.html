About IKPCONNECT

IKPCONNECT is an annual conference organized by the Kurdish Institute of Paris, dedicated to promoting collaboration across various academic and research fields. Its main goal is to introduce new researchers and connect them with experienced scholars working in similar areas. The event also serves as a platform for emerging researchers to present their work, showcase their progress, and become part of a growing academic community.
